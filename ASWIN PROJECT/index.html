Fram To Home

We manufacture milk and milk products with our pioneering technology and strict standards. We tend to deliver farm fresh milk and milk products to every homes.

Step:1 Milking

Our Farmers Use Milking Machines To Milk their cows 2 times per day. It only takes 10 minutes for a cow to be milked! Some farmers milk in a parlor, while some have adopted robotic milker technology – both are safe, quick and stress-free ways to milk cows.

Step:2 pasteurization

The most common method of pasteurization is High Temperature Short Time (HTST) pasteurization, which uses metal plates and hot water to raise milk temperatures to at least 161° F for not less than 15 seconds, followed by rapid cooling.

Step:3 Packing

The packaging of milk is an important aspect to protect, carry, and market, to sustain its freshness and convenience.We are used for packaging milk, such as bottles/containers, cans, cartons, and pouches/bags. oru packaging trends is respect to our country and region. There are several reasons to ensure the durable packaging of milk, like safety, information, ease of storage and handling, and avoiding distribution damages.

Step:4 Delivery To You

Once Our Products Received From The Vethaa Our Sales Excutive Check's The Products Have any Leakages Or Any Damages During The Transport. Then We Sale That products To The Our Valuable Customers.
